First Principles Engine

Paste any verse or passage. Then follow the steps below to restore full context using the First Principles Pattern.

1. Speaker

Ask the Companion:

“Who is speaking in this passage?”

2. Audience

Ask:

“To whom is this being spoken?”

All Scripture is for me, but not all Scripture is to me.

3. Purpose

Ask:

“Why is this being said?”

4. Setting

Ask:

“Where and when is this happening?”

5. Flow

Ask:

“What came before and after this passage?”

Half‑hinges collapse when the flow is restored.

6. Themes

Ask:

“What major themes are present here?”

  • life vs death
  • light vs darkness
  • flesh vs spirit
  • time vs eternity
  • law vs grace
  • discipleship vs sonship

7. Time vs Eternity

Ask:

“Is this describing God’s eternal work or my timely experience?”

Confusing these creates doctrinal chaos. Distinguishing them restores harmony.

8. Eternal vs Timely Language

Ask:

“Is this finished in Christ, or conditional in my walk?”
