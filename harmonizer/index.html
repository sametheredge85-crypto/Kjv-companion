About KJV Harmonizer

KJV Harmonizer is a Bible study tool designed to help you understand the Scriptures through the lens of harmony.

Our Mission

Unbiased whole counsel, whole context, wholly harmonious. We believe that Scripture interprets Scripture, and by viewing parallel passages together, we can gain deeper understanding of God's Word.

Features

  • Gospel Harmony: Compare parallel passages from all four Gospels side by side
  • Parallel View: Display any two passages for comparison
  • Search: Find verses containing specific words or phrases
  • Bookmarks: Save your favorite verses and add notes
  • Offline Access: Works without internet connection

King James Version

This app uses the King James Version (KJV) of the Holy Bible, first published in 1611 and beloved for its majestic language and faithful translation.
