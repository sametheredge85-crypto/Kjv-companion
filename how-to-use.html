How to Use This Tool

This tool is designed to help you explore the whole counsel of God using the King James Bible alone. It is non-denominational, unbiased, and focused entirely on Scripture in its full context.

1. Explore the Annex Library

The Annex contains harmonized studies, sermon entries, and doctrinal notes. Each entry is presented in full context — speaker, audience, purpose, and surrounding passages.

Go to the Annex Index and select any entry to begin.

2. Load Your Own Sermons or Notes

To add your own material, open the Annex Loader page. Paste your sermon, notes, or doctrinal block into the Companion and say:

“Convert this into an Annex entry.”

You will receive a formatted HTML file ready to save into your library.

3. Search for Topics

Use the search bar on the Annex Index page to type any topic, passage, or theme. Then copy your search phrase and ask the Companion:

“Search the Annex Library for [your topic].”

4. Understand the Harmony

This tool does not cherry-pick verses or rely on isolated proof-texts. It restores every passage to its full setting — for example, John 3:1–20 — and lets Scripture interpret Scripture.

If something does not harmonize, the tool will state it plainly and explain why, so honest study can solidify the facts.

5. Trust the Text, Not Tradition

This tool does not promote a system, group, or denomination. It simply reveals the consistency already present in the KJV. Truth remains true whether we believed it or not.

6. Add Audio, Images, and Visuals

You may include sermon audio, hymn recordings, Scripture images, motifs, and templates in your repository. These can be linked from any page to enrich study and teaching.
