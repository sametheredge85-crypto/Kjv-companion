John 3:16 — New Birth & Eternal Life

Verse

“For God so loved the world...” — John 3:16

Context

Eternal love before the foundation → a particular people given to the Son → sovereign new birth produces belief and fruit.

Harmony

  • John 17:1–2 — give eternal life to as many as thou hast given him
  • Matthew 1:21 — save his people from their sins
  • Ephesians 1:4 — chosen us in him before the foundation of the world
  • John 17:24 — thou lovedst me before the foundation of the world
  • 1 Peter 1:20–22 — foreordained before the foundation... believing... purified... unfeigned love
  • John 6:29 — This is the work of God, that ye believe
  • Philippians 1:29 — it is given... to believe on him
  • Romans 12:1–3 — transformed by the renewing of your mind... measure of faith
  • Galatians 5:22–23 — fruit of the Spirit: love, joy, peace...
  • Revelation 17:8 — names... in the book of life from the foundation of the world
