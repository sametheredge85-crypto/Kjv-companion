How to Ask for a Whole‑Counsel Answer

After typing your question, copy it and ask the Companion:

“Answer this using the whole counsel of the KJV in full context.”

The Companion will:

  • restore the entire passage (not just a verse)
  • identify speaker, audience, purpose, and setting
  • harmonize related passages across the KJV
  • avoid cherry‑picking or half‑hinge reasoning
  • explain why certain interpretations collapse under context
  • give a consistent, non‑denominational answer

Why This Works

Most doctrinal confusion — including universalism — depends on half‑verses, isolated phrases, or ignoring the speaker and audience. When the whole passage is restored, the harmony becomes clear and cannot be disproven.

This tool does not promote a system. It simply reveals the consistency already present in the KJV.
