About This Tool

This tool exists for one purpose: to present the whole counsel of God — complete, contextual, and harmonized — using the King James Bible as its only standard.

It is non-denominational. It does not promote a system, a tradition, or a group. It simply lets Scripture interpret Scripture, allowing the full context, audience, setting, and flow of the text to speak for itself.

Harmonizing the KJV may challenge long-held assumptions. This is not because of opinion, bias, or background, but because truth remains true whether we believed it or not.

Independent AI platforms consistently align on these harmonies for one reason: the text is self-consistent. When the whole context is considered — such as John 3:1–20 — the meaning becomes clear without cherry-picking or half-hinge reasoning.

This tool distinguishes between what God has done for His people and what we experience in time. It does not flatten Scripture; it clarifies it. If something does not harmonize, the tool acknowledges it and explains why, so reasonable study can solidify the facts.
